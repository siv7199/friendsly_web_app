# Supabase Confirmation Email Template

Use this in Supabase Auth for the confirmation email template. The template itself is managed in the Supabase dashboard, not in this repo.

Suggested subject:

`Confirm your Friendsly account`

Suggested heading:

`Friendsly`

Suggested body copy:

`Press the link below to confirm your email and finish setting up your Friendsly account.`

Suggested CTA:

`Press the link to confirm your email`

Suggested footer copy:

`Once you confirm your email, Friendsly will send you back to the sign-in page so you can continue.`

Notes:

- Use an email-safe font stack that approximates the brand instead of relying on the app's web font.
- Keep the CTA copy aligned with the in-app sign-up confirmation modal on [app/login/page.tsx](/c:/Users/chint/Downloads/Friendsly_web_app/app/login/page.tsx:445).
- Dashboard path: `Supabase Dashboard -> Auth -> Templates -> Confirm signup`
